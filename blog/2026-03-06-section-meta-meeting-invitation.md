We warmly invite all NFDI members and other interested parties to join the upcoming **Section Meta meeting** on **31 March 2026 at 4:00 pm (CET)**.

- **Updates from the Working Groups**

The meeting is open to everyone with an interest in research data metadata and NFDI infrastructure. We look forward to seeing you there!

For connection details, please watch for the meeting announcement on the Section Meta mailing list.
