# Working Group Terminology Services

Terminologies are at the heart of FAIR metadata for research data management (RDM). Terminologies have various characteristics: they describe domain knowledge (e.g. chemistry, biology, and information science use their own terminologies) and they constantly evolve over time, requiring acceptance and coordination (mapping) within their knowledge domain and beyond.

The overarching goal of the Terminology Services working group is to **drive joint developments, share best practices, and reduce barriers to the use of terminologies** across NFDI consortia.
