# Working Group Knowledge Transfer and Impact

This working group collaborates with companies to translate research into concrete measures. It jointly develops solutions for industrial practice with partners, implements them, and measures their success.

The group bridges the gap between scientific output and real-world industrial application by:

- Identifying research results from NFDI consortia with direct industrial relevance
- Co-developing and piloting implementation strategies with industry partners
- Establishing metrics and methods to assess the impact of science–industry knowledge transfer
- Documenting and disseminating successful transfer cases as replicable models
