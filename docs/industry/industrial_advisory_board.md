# Industrial Advisory Board (Industriebeirat)

The Industrial Advisory Board ensures that the perspectives and interests of industry are substantively incorporated into the NFDI section's work. It brings together a broad spectrum of companies — including DAX corporations, SMEs, and startups — from various industrial sectors. The board is set up as heterogeneously as possible with respect to company size, sector affiliation, and industrial focus, to ensure a wide range of perspectives.
