# Working Group Cooperation Models and Cooperation Agreements

This working group analyses forms of cooperation between industry and academic research specifically within the NFDI, and develops a collection of **industry-specific model contracts** for research cooperations — taking into account important aspects such as data reuse.

Exchange with industry partners helps to better understand their interests and to promote interaction through workshops and meetings. A stakeholder analysis supports networking, with synergies possible with other working groups.
