# Working Group Benefits, Costs, and Risks in Research Cooperations

This working group acts as a bridge for the sustainable networking of science and industry. It develops approaches to promote collaboration between the two sides, with a focus on data use and data sharing.

The group's work encompasses:

- Conducting a **needs analysis** from both the science and industry perspectives
- **Validating practical examples** of existing cooperations
- Developing a **benefit-risk balance** framework applicable to cross-sector collaborations
- Using **focus groups** to develop, generalise, and legally and contractually underpin concrete projects and scenarios
