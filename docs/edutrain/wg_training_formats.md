# Working Group Training Formats and Certificate Courses

In addition to the open provision of teaching materials in the Knowledge Base, this working group develops more formalised training in the form of **certificate courses** — for example, for Data Stewards.

Many stakeholders not only want to acquire data competence as a meta-competence, but also want to have it confirmed by external and recognised bodies. Certificate courses also ensure a comparable and consistent quality in training.
