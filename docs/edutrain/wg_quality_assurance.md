# Working Group Quality Assurance and Evaluation

In close coordination with the [Target Group and Needs Analysis](./wg_target_analysis) WG, this working group develops a quality assurance concept for the teaching offerings. The concept aims to motivate authors to regularly reflect on whether an update or adaptation of teaching content is needed due to changing needs.

Users are offered low-threshold feedback mechanisms to evaluate how well materials match the intended target group and how they are being used. In the spirit of a Train-the-Trainer approach, low-threshold opportunities for collaboration are also provided so that individuals can actively improve specific components they use — benefiting from the practical experience of users and the respective target group.
