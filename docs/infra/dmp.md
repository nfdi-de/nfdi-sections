# Working Group Data Management Planning (DMP)

The working group aims to coordinate the DMP activities of the NFDI consortia. In addition to establishing DMP tools, coordinating their further development, and harmonising the content of DMP templates, the group is also developing concepts for the strategic implementation of DMPs in research workflows, training and consulattion services. Since DMPs form essential links or intermediate components of the data management workflow and cover almost its entire content, the working group seeks partial cooperation with other NFDI sections such as EduTrain and Metadata.
