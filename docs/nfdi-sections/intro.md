## NFDI Sections Roadshow

:::tip Watch the recordings
The **NFDI Sections Roadshow** took place on **4 February 2026**. Recordings are freely available on YouTube.

**[▶ Watch the Roadshow on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EVmgK0HH7xU)**
:::

The Roadshow brought together all six NFDI sections to present their work and engage with the broader NFDI community. It explored the cross-cutting themes that shape a national research data infrastructure by addressing three central questions:

- **What are the key cross-cutting topics** for a research data infrastructure?
- **What challenges do the sections face** in building this infrastructure on a national scale?
- **What solutions are the sections developing** to meet these challenges?
